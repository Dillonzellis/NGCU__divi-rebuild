Savasaurus Kids Club

NGCU makes saving fun for young members and offers the Savasaurus Kids Club for members age 12 and under.

Other features of the Savasaurus Kids Club include:

  • Teach youth the value of saving and borrowing
  • Events, contests, giveaways and visits by Sunny the Savasaurus
  • Free membership kit includes pocket folder, book cover, coloring book, pencil, ruler, eraser and membership card

Please contact us if you have any questions regarding the Savasaurus Kids Club.
