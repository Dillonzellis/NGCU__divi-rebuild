Checking Accounts

Once you have opened a regular share/savings account at NGCU, you become eligible to apply for a Checking Account and receive our many
benefits.

Features of Checking Accounts include:

  • No minimum balance requirement
  • No monthly fee
  • No per-check fee
  • No-fee NGCU ATMs at four convenient locations and ATM services via STAR®, CIRRUS®, PLUS® VISA®and MasterCard®
  • FREE VISA®Debit Card
  • Consolidated monthly statements mailed or e-Statements
  • FREE home banking and free bill pay when you choose e-Statements
  • FREE bill pay when you choose e-Statements
  • Overdraft protection with pre-approved line of credit
  • Direct Deposit & Payroll Deduction

Please see our Fee Statement for any applicable fees, or click here for a checking account application.
