Scholarships

North Georgia Credit Union (NGCU) offers a $1,000 scholarship to a member of NGCU, or the child of a NGCU member. The scholarships are granted to one student from each of the following schools: Stephens County High School, Franklin County High School, Hart County High School and Rabun County High School. The scholarship committee awards a high school senior based on need, grades, SAT/ACT scores, community and school projects and written recommendations. The scholarship committee reviews the applications after the deadline of March 6, 2023. Application packets will not be accepted after this date, or prior to January 23, 2023.
