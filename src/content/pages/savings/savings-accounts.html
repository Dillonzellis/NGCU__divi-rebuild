Savings Accounts

To become a member of North Georgia Credit Union (NGCU), a Regular Share/Savings Account must be opened in your name. An initial deposit of $25 is required plus a $5 one time membership fee. All members must maintain a $25 balance in their savings account to remain a member, and demonstrate regular activity in their account to keep it open.
